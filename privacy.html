Privacy Policy

Effective Date: November 18th, 2024

Introduction

1000 Hills Rugby values your privacy and complies with Rwanda's privacy laws. This policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your personal information when you visit our website (https://1000hillsrugby.rw), engage with our services, or interact with us.

Your Rights

We use your personal information only for the purposes stated in this policy. If you disagree with this policy, you should stop using our services and website.

Information We Collect

We may collect the following personal information:

  • Your name, contact details, and any information you provide directly (e.g., during registrations or inquiries).
  • Information about your use of our website, such as pages visited and time spent.
  • Data from surveys or feedback.

Please do not provide information about others unless they consent to this and are aware of this policy.

How We Collect Your Information

We collect personal information:

  • Directly from you (e.g., when you fill out forms).
  • Indirectly through your website use or social media interactions.
  • From third parties, such as analytics services.

How We Use and Share Your Information

Your information is used for the following purposes:

  • To provide services, process donations, or manage registrations.
  • To improve our website functionality and user experience.
  • To respond to your inquiries or feedback.
  • To comply with legal obligations.

We will not sell or rent your personal data. Information may be shared with service providers only for operational purposes.

Cookies

We use cookies to enhance your browsing experience. You can adjust your browser settings to disable cookies, though this may impact some features on the site.

Third-Party Links

Our website may include links to third-party sites. These sites operate independently and have their own privacy policies, for which we are not responsible.

Your Rights to Access and Correction

You have the right to access and correct your personal information. If you believe your data is incorrect, you may contact us at info@1000hillsrugby.rw.

Changes to This Privacy Policy

This policy may be updated periodically. Any changes will be posted on our website and will take effect 14 days after posting. Continued use of our website or services implies acceptance of the updated terms.

Contact Us

For privacy-related inquiries, please email info@1000hillsrugby.rw.
