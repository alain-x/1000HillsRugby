Leaders of the Game

Experience, Leadership, and Legacy

At 1000 Hills Rugby, we take pride in our experienced members who bring years of leadership, wisdom, and dedication to the field. Our team members, both on and off the field, are not just athletes but also mentors, role models, and key figures in our community. Their commitment to the game and fostering a positive environment creates a lasting impact, inspiring the next generation. Watch their stories and see how they continue to shape the future of rugby and our community.

Empowering Girls through Rugby

Strength, Courage, and Support!

At 1000 Hills Rugby, we are dedicated to celebrating and empowering girls through the transformative power of sport. Our girls' program goes beyond athletic development; it fosters confidence, teamwork, and community involvement, creating a holistic environment where young women can thrive. We believe that by participating in rugby, girls can break barriers, challenge stereotypes, and develop the leadership skills necessary to excel both on and off the field. Our program is designed to inspire and support female athletes, encouraging them to embrace their strength and determination while forging lasting friendships within a supportive community.

We invite you to join us in championing our female athletes as they showcase their skills and resilience. Through rigorous training, mentorship, and competitive opportunities, we equip young women with the tools they need to succeed in rugby and life. At 1000 Hills Rugby, we are committed to nurturing the next generation of female leaders, empowering them to make a positive impact in their communities and beyond. Together, let’s celebrate the strength, courage, and spirit of our girls as they rise to new heights in the world of rugby!

Inspiring and Uplifting Our Youth

Building Future Leaders!

At 1000 Hills Rugby, we are committed to empowering young people through sport and beyond. We focus on cultivating leadership, teamwork, and resilience in every young athlete. Each training session, event, and activity is an opportunity to build character and instill lifelong values. Together, we are shaping the next generation of leaders who will carry forward the spirit and strength of our community, both on and off the field.
