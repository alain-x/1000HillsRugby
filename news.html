RUGBY, NEWS

Heroes Rugby Cup 2025: A Celebration of Sport and Legacy

1 FEBRUARY 2025

Last Saturday, February 1, 2025, the second edition of the Heroes Rugby Cup concluded in Kicukiro District. Organized by 1000 Hills Rugby, this tournament responded to the lack of competitive opportunities for rugby players in Rwanda. It aimed to keep players engaged, showcase their talents, and honor the Heroes of our Nation each year.

Senior Rugby Clash: Kalisimbi RFC vs. Muhabura RFC The highlight of the day was the highly anticipated match between Kalisimbi RFC and Muhabura RFC. Kalisimbi delivered a dominant performance, securing a 31-5 victory with exceptional plays, strong defense, and impressive teamwork. Despite Muhabura’s resilience, Kalisimbi controlled the game with fast-paced attacks and solid execution.


Youth and Grassroots Rugby in Action

The tournament wasn’t just about senior rugby. The Mixed Boys (T1 Rugby) and Girls teams, along with the U12 age group (Tag Rugby), displayed incredible skills and enthusiasm. These young players are the future of Rwandan rugby, and their participation reflected the growing talent in grassroots rugby development.

Beyond the Game: Honoring Rwanda’s Heroes
The Heroes Rugby Cup is more than just a competition—it is a tribute to Rwanda’s national heroes, emphasizing the values of courage, resilience, and teamwork. Through rugby, we continue to inspire and empower young athletes while fostering a sense of unity and national pride.

A Big Thank You!

We extend our gratitude to all the teams, coaches, referees, and supporters who made this event a success. Special thanks to our partners and sponsors who continue to support the development of rugby in Rwanda.


Stay tuned for more upcoming tournaments and events as we build on this momentum!

Shalom already looking ahead for his new position as 'Safeguarding Officer'

17 JANUARY 2025

We are excited to announce that Shalom is taking on his new role as Safeguarding Officer at 1000 Hills Rugby! Over the past 10 years, Shalom has grown with us, starting from tag rugby to the development team, then the senior team, and now as a coach for the development team. He is a true example of success and dedication. As he said in his words, "I will work with the team to create a safe environment for children and everyone in our programs, and I will ensure our policies are clear and understood by all our stakeholders. We are proud of Shalom and his commitment to making a positive impact!

The Women National League, One of a kind for our women's team

9 JANUARY 2025

For years, we dreamed of the day our women’s team would take their place on the big stage. That day has finally come! For the first time ever, 1000 Hills Rugby will be represented in the Women Nation League. It’s hard to believe that not long ago, many of these players were too young to participate, and we didn’t even have a senior women’s team to compete. But through passion, perseverance, and countless hours of hard work, these incredible women have grown into a team ready to make history. This isn’t just about rugby—it’s about breaking barriers and showing the power of determination. The journey begins now, and we couldn’t be prouder!
