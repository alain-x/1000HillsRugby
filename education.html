Welcome to
1000 Hills Rugby Education Program

Introduction

The 1000 Hills Rugby Education Program is dedicated to empowering young girls and boys by providing them with educational opportunities that can transform their lives. This initiative emerged from our engagement with young rugby players, many of whom come from underserved backgrounds.

We recognize the financial hardships they face, which can hinder their ability to continue their education. Through this program, we seek to eliminate these barriers and ensure that every child has the opportunity to pursue an education and a brighter future.

Vision

    Our vision is to create a society where every child, irrespective of gender or financial circumstances, has equal access to quality education. We believe education is not just a fundamental right but also a transformative force that drives personal growth, empowerment, and sustainable societal progress.

Mission

    Our mission is to mobilize support from friends, families, and organizations committed to investing in the education of young rugby players. By highlighting the challenges these children face, we aim to inspire collective action and create sustainable opportunities for them to access quality education.

Purpose

    The purpose of the 1000 Hills Rugby Education Program is to eliminate financial obstacles that hinder children's access to education. Many families face significant economic hardships, making it difficult to afford school fees and essential learning materials. Our program seeks to provide financial aid, educational resources, and mentorship to ensure these children not only stay in school but also thrive academically and personally.

Approach

    We take a holistic approach to education, recognizing that it involves more than just financial assistance. While we aim to cover school fees, we also prioritize mentorship, life skills development, and emotional support. We encourage sponsors to form meaningful connections with the children they support, serving as mentors and role models. Additionally, we offer opportunities for sponsors to engage in video calls and interactive sessions, fostering a nurturing and empowering community.

How to Support

    If you are willing to make a difference in the lives of these children, we invite you to join our cause. Your contributions, no matter how small, can have a profound impact. For example, a donation of $50 can help one player receive a school bag and essential stationery for a year, ensuring they have the necessary materials to support their education. We provide sponsors with the child’s details, family contact information, and the school's bank account details for direct transfers. Together, we can give these children the educational opportunities they deserve.

The Role of Rugby

    As part of our program, supported children will have the opportunity to engage in rugby, which is at the core of 1000 Hills Rugby’s mission. While participation is encouraged but not mandatory, we recognize the transformative power of rugby. The sport fosters discipline, teamwork, resilience, and leadership—skills that benefit young players both on and off the field. By integrating rugby into our education program, we not only enhance their physical well-being but also instill confidence and a sense of belonging in a structured, supportive environment.

Our Achievements

Over the years, the 1000 Hills Rugby Education Program has grown significantly. We began by supporting just 10 players, and through continued effort and community support, we have expanded our impact to 65 beneficiaries under the SESLA program. This achievement demonstrates our dedication to providing educational opportunities for young athletes.

Education Support Breakdown

  • Total Registered Members: 123
  • Total Beneficiaries under SESLA: 65
    • Girls Supported: 35
    • Boys Supported: 30

Testimony from a Beneficiary

    “Before joining the 1000 Hills Rugby Education Program, I struggled to continue my Advanced Level studies due to financial constraints. Joining 1000 Hills Rugby gave me an opportunity to be part of this program, which helped me continue my education.

    Thanks to this initiative, I have been able to go back to school, receive school supplies, and develop my rugby skills. The support I have received has given me hope for a better future, both academically and in sports.” — IRADUKUNDA IGIHOZO KEVINE, 18 Years, G.S. GATENGA

Conclusion

    The 1000 Hills Rugby Education Program is committed to breaking the cycle of limited opportunities for children from underserved communities. We believe that education is a powerful tool that can unlock potential, uplift communities, and create lasting change. By providing financial assistance, fostering personal connections, and promoting rugby participation, we aim to empower these children to become future leaders and role models. Together, let's support their education, transform lives, and build a more equitable society.
