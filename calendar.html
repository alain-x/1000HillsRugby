Apr 19, 2025 1HR Genocide Memorial Visit Members, Senior Men and Women Teams, Youth U18, U16 & U12 1HR, Nyanza Genocide Memorial / Kicukiro / Gatenga This initiative reflects our commitment to fostering unity, resilience, and positive values among our youth. By reflecting on the tragic events of the 1994 Genocide against the Tutsi, we aim to educate and empower our players to stand firm against division and promote a message of reconciliation and solidarity. This visit is not just about honoring the past; it’s about equipping our members with the mindset to contribute to a brighter future for Rwanda. It also serves as a mirror for the international community, urging all of us to stand strong and ensure that "Never Again" becomes a reality worldwide. Annually 1000 Hills Rugby players, coaches, and management. Partners, stakeholders, and supporters of unity and peace-building initiatives. Through this visit, we reaffirm our role as ambassadors of unity, using rugby as a platform to inspire change and strengthen our shared commitment to peace and development.
