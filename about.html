The Journey of 1000 Hills Rugby: Empowering Communities Through Sport

Welcome to the home of 1000 Hills Rugby! Here, we share our story, our mission, and the milestones that define our journey of transforming lives through rugby.

Learn More

Our Roots

1000 Hills Rugby was born in 2014 at Don Bosco Gatenga with a bold vision: to harness the power of rugby as a unifying force. Our aim was to create a safe and enjoyable environment where young athletes could grow their skills, deepen their understanding of the game, and play rugby to their fullest potential. Recognizing the challenges faced by Rwanda’s youth, we combined sports with education to tackle employability and ensure sustainable livelihoods.

Starting with just three Level 1 coaches and 20 U18 boys, we quickly expanded through the World Rugby’s "Get Into Rugby" program. By early 2016, we had grown into a thriving community of 25 senior members, 20 U18 women, and 20 U16 boys. Currently, we have over 150 beneficiaries in our programs.

Our Mission

  • To develop our players into the best men’s, women’s, and youth teams they can be.
  • To establish a supportive sporting home for individuals of all ages.
  • To be the home of winning teams in Rwanda.

Our Vision

  • To be the heart of rugby in Rwanda.
  • To prepare players as bright competitors on the international rugby stage.
  • To cultivate disciplined, active members who contribute to Rwanda’s economic and social development.

Our Core Values

  1. Discipline: The foundation of excellence.
  2. Sportsmanship: Playing with respect and fairness.
  3. Mutuality: Building strong relationships within the rugby community.
  4. Integrity: Acting with honesty and transparency.
  5. Mental and Physical Strength: Building resilience on and off the field.

Milestones Achieved

  • In 2016, we proudly contributed five players to Rwanda Rugby’s National Team for the African Rugby Cup Division 2 South.
  • We won the Rwanda Rugby National League in 2016.
  • Champions of the Kigali 7s Tournament in 2017 and 2018.
  • Winners of the Genocide Memorial Tournament in 2016, 2017, and 2018.
  • Hosted the Rugby 7s tournament from 2016 to 2018 to strengthen regional competition.
  • The first two editions were won by Remera Buffaloes, while 1000 Hills Rugby claimed victory in the third edition.

While challenges delayed subsequent events, we are thrilled to announce the return of this prestigious tournament in December 2025.

Community Impact

At 1000 Hills Rugby, our influence extends far beyond the pitch. Through our annual charity event, 1000 Hills Rugby Voice, we promote healthy lifestyles and community development.

During the COVID-19 pandemic, we mobilized our global supporters to sustain our programs, ensuring our mission endured.

In 2023-2024, we launched the initiative "Building Stronger Bonds: Uniting Families through Rugby for Future Leaders." This program strengthens player-family relationships, fostering deeper connections and support.

Visiting 26 families of U18 boys and girls in Kicukiro District, we have seen higher attendance, stronger parental involvement, and a brighter future for our young athletes.

The Road Ahead

Looking ahead, our focus remains on empowering Rwanda’s youth through sports, education, and employability programs. By leveraging rugby as a tool for positive change, we aim to tackle the challenges faced by young people and create a sustainable future for our community.

Thriving with 1000 Hills Rugby

Real-world Impact

At 1000 Hills Rugby, we help young people face real-life challenges with confidence through teamwork and hands-on learning. Our programs build important skills like leadership, teamwork, and discipline, while also teaching rugby and life values. With guidance from mentors, support from our community, and opportunities to grow, we prepare our players to succeed both on and off the field and make a positive impact in their lives and communities.

From Grassroots to Greatness

Program Structure and Pathways

At 1000 Hills Rugby, our program helps players grow from beginners to their full potential by combining rugby training with life and job readiness skills. We focus on building strong foundations, developing advanced techniques, and teaching valuable skills like teamwork, leadership, and communication. Players train hard, learn from experienced coaches, and apply their abilities both on the field and in real-world situations. By the end of the program, they are prepared to compete at higher levels and succeed in their careers and personal lives.
