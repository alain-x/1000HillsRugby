Elite Player Development Program

Description

This program targets the club’s top players and prepares them for high-performance rugby, with a focus on both Rugby XV and Rugby 7s. Age Group: 18+ years

Key Features

Intensive training and conditioning programs Access to professional-level coaching Tactical and technical development for elite competition Exposure to national and international scouts and competitions



Women’s Rugby Program

Description

Dedicated to empowering women through rugby, this program encourages females of all ages to participate in the sport, whether as beginners or experienced players. Age Group: 16+ years

Key Features

Regular training sessions and fitness programs Participation in local and regional women’s tournaments Leadership and personal development workshops Mentorship from experienced players



Grassroots Rugby Initiative

Description

This program integrates rugby training with life skills workshops, helping players develop both athletically and personally. Age Group: 12-20 years

Key Features

Rugby training combined with leadership, communication, and teamwork workshops Sessions on financial literacy, health and wellness, and career development Mentorship from senior players and community leaders Focus on character building and real-world skills



Grassroots Rugby Initiative

Description

This outreach program aims to bring rugby to underserved communities, providing training, equipment, and opportunities for young people to engage with the sport. Age Group: 6-15 years
