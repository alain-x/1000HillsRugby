Elite Player Development Program

This program targets the club’s top players and prepares them for high-performance rugby, focusing on both Rugby XV and Rugby 7s. Age Group: 18+ years

  • Intensive training and conditioning programs
  • Access to professional-level coaching
  • Tactical and technical development for elite competition
  • Exposure to national and international scouts

Women’s Rugby Program

Dedicated to empowering women through rugby, this program encourages females of all ages to participate in the sport. Age Group: 16+ years

  • Regular training sessions and fitness programs
  • Participation in local and regional tournaments
  • Leadership and personal development workshops
  • Mentorship from experienced players

Rugby & Life Skills Program

This initiative integrates rugby training with life skills workshops, helping players develop athletically and personally. Age Group: 12-20 years

  • Leadership, communication, and teamwork workshops
  • Financial literacy, health, and career development sessions
  • Mentorship from senior players and community leaders
  • Focus on character building and real-world skills

Grassroots Rugby Initiative

This outreach program aims to bring rugby to underserved communities, providing training, equipment, and opportunities for young people to engage with the sport. Age Group: 6-15 years

  • Rugby introduction clinics in schools and communities
  • Free access to equipment and training
  • Collaboration with local schools to promote rugby education
  • Talent scouting for entry into the Youth Development Program
