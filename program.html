Player Development Program

At 1000 Hills Rugby, we believe in nurturing talent both on and off the field, and our Player Development Program is at the heart of this mission. This program is designed to help players grow their skills, build confidence, and reach their full potential. From technical rugby training to personal development sessions, we ensure our players are prepared for success in the game and beyond. It’s more than just rugby—it’s about empowering individuals with the tools they need to excel in life. Together, we are building a strong foundation for future champions. Age Group: 18+ years

  • Enhancing technical and tactical rugby skills
  • Building strength, endurance, and agility
  • Offering workshops on leadership, teamwork, communication, and life skills
  • Preparing players for opportunities within and outside of rugby

Women’s Rugby Program

Empowering Women Through Rugby

At 1000 Hills Rugby, we believe in the transformative power of sports to inspire, uplift, and empower women. Our Women’s Rugby Program is thoughtfully designed to create opportunities for females aged 12 and above to thrive both on and off the field.

Who Can Join?
Age Group: 12+ years.

Open to beginners and experienced players alike, our program welcomes all women looking to enhance their rugby skills and personal development.

What We Offer:

    Regular Training and Fitness Programs
  • Train Like a Champion: Participate in weekly sessions focused on sharpening rugby skills and boosting overall fitness, setting players up for success on the field.
  • Customized for Excellence: Tailored training plans ensure that every player, from beginner to advanced, achieves their full potential.

    • Education and Employability Opportunities
  • Learn, Grow, Succeed: Engage in workshops that build essential skills for academic achievement and career advancement.
  • Your Future, Empowered: Access mentorship and career guidance that opens doors to limitless opportunities.
  • Supporting Education: We facilitate school fees and provide necessary materials such as books, bags, and other essentials to ensure our players can excel both in the classroom and on the field.

    • Competitive Play
  • Compete, Win, Inspire: Take part in thrilling local and regional tournaments, building teamwork and a winning mindset
  • On the World Stage: Represent the club and Rwanda with pride, showcasing your talent on national and international platforms.

    • Leadership and Personal Development
  • Leaders On and Off the Field: Develop leadership skills that inspire teammates and create positive change beyond rugby.
  • Confidence Unlocked: Participate in workshops that strengthen resilience and prepare you to tackle life’s challenges.

    • Mentorship
  • Guidance for Greatness: Learn from experienced players and coaches who provide invaluable support for both personal and athletic growth.
  • Inspiring the Next Generation: Receive mentorship that shapes you into a role model for future players.

Whether you’re looking to play competitively, improve your fitness, or simply be part of a supportive community, the Women’s Rugby Program is your platform to grow, learn, and shine. Join us in empowering women through rugby! REGISTER NOW

Rugby & Life Skills Program

Empowering Future Leaders Through Rugby

At 1000 Hills Rugby, we understand that rugby is more than just a game; it is a powerful tool for personal and community development. Our Rugby & Life Skills Program is specifically designed for players aged 10 and above, combining high-quality rugby training with essential life skills education. This holistic approach ensures that our participants not only excel in their athletic pursuits but also grow into well-rounded individuals ready to face the challenges of life.

Program Highlights:

  • Inclusive Participation Open to boys and girls of all skill levels, our program welcomes everyone from senior teams to development squads and juniors, fostering a supportive and inclusive environment.

  • Comprehensive Workshops Participants engage in workshops focused on leadership, communication, and teamwork, where they learn to lead with integrity and collaborate effectively with others.

  • Practical Life Skills Our curriculum includes sessions on financial literacy, health, and career development, equipping players with the knowledge and skills necessary for making informed life choices and achieving their professional aspirations.

  • Mentorship OpportunitiesPlayers benefit from mentorship by senior players and community leaders, gaining valuable insights and guidance that inspire personal and professional growth.

  • Character Development We emphasize the importance of discipline, integrity, and resilience, helping players navigate challenges both on and off the field while preparing them to be active contributors to society.

Why Choose Our Program?
The Rugby & Life Skills Program at 1000 Hills Rugby is committed to nurturing disciplined, empowered individuals who embody the values of sportsmanship, mutual respect, and community engagement. By integrating athletic training with life skills education, we prepare our players to become leaders and role models, ready to represent Rwanda with pride on the international stage.

Grassroots Rugby Initiative

Bringing Rugby to Every Corner of Rwanda

At 1000 Hills Rugby, we believe that every child deserves the opportunity to discover the joy of rugby and the invaluable life skills it imparts. Our Grassroots Rugby Initiative is dedicated to introducing the sport to underserved communities, breaking down barriers, and nurturing future talent. This program aligns with our mission to foster inclusivity, develop youth, and empower communities through the transformative power of rugby.

Who Can Join?

  • Age Group: 6–15 years
  • Open to boys and girls in schools and communities with limited access to sports programs, our initiative welcomes all young aspiring athletes.

What We Offer:

    Rugby Introduction Clinics in Schools and Communities
  • Engaging Learning Experiences:
  • Expert Coaching: Age-appropriate training delivered by experienced coaches to ensure a positive introduction to the sport.

    • Free Access to Equipment and Training
  • Essential Gear Provided: We supply rugby balls, kits, and other necessary equipment to ensure that all participants can engage fully in the program.
  • Inclusive Training Environment: Our sessions prioritize safety, teamwork, and sportsmanship, creating a welcoming atmosphere for all.

    • Collaboration with Local Schools to Promote Rugby Education
  • Partnerships for Progress: We work closely with schools to integrate rugby into their physical education programs, enhancing the overall sports curriculum.
  • Teacher Workshops: Hosting workshops for educators to equip them with the tools and knowledge to support long-term rugby education in their schools.

    • Facilitating Education Support
  • Comprehensive Assistance: We provide school fees and essential materials such as books, bags, and uniforms to ensure that all participants can thrive both academically and athletically.
  • Holistic Development: Our goal is to ensure that young players have the resources they need to succeed in all aspects of their lives.

    • Talent Scouting for the Youth Development Program
  • Identifying Future Stars: We actively scout and nurture young talent, providing pathways for skilled players to advance into our Youth Development Program for continued growth and competitive play.

Why Join?
The Grassroots Rugby Initiative aims to inspire the next generation of players by instilling core values of discipline, mutual respect, and teamwork. By making rugby accessible to all, we empower young people to become confident, active members of their communities, ready to embrace the challenges of life both on and off the field.
