1000 Hills Rugby Career Guidance Program

Empowering youth through rugby and career development

Building Future Leaders

Combining sports training with essential life skills for a brighter future

Creating Opportunities

Pathways to employment and entrepreneurship through our program

At 1000 Hills Rugby, we use rugby as a tool to unlock potential and prepare youth for successful futures. Our Career Guidance Program empowers boys and girls with life skills, job readiness, and entrepreneurship training in a safe, inclusive environment.

Through hands-on workshops and expert-led sessions, participants build confidence, set goals, and develop leadership, teamwork, communication, CV writing, interview, and critical thinking skills. We help youth discover their passions, whether in sports, business, or other careers, and prepare them to thrive.

Our Skills Development

We follow a structured approach to equip youth with essential skills for life and work

Confidence Building

Developing self-assurance through achievement, positive reinforcement, and overcoming challenges on and off the field.

Goal Setting

Learning to set SMART goals and develop strategies to achieve personal, academic, and professional objectives.

Leadership

Cultivating the ability to guide, inspire and motivate others through rugby team roles and community projects.

Teamwork

Mastering collaboration, conflict resolution and collective achievement through rugby and group initiatives.

Communication

Developing effective verbal, non-verbal and digital communication skills for professional environments.

CV Writing

Creating professional resumes, cover letters and online profiles that effectively showcase skills and experience.

Interview Skills

Mastering the art of professional interviews, from preparation to follow-up, through mock sessions and feedback.

Critical Thinking

Developing analytical abilities, problem-solving techniques and decision-making skills for real-world challenges.

Continuous Growth

Applying all learned skills in real-world scenarios while developing habits for lifelong learning and adaptation.
